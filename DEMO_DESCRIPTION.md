### Interactive Elements

When you click any "Try" button:
1. The page dims with a semi-transparent overlay
2. The chosen loader appears in the center
3. Smooth fade-in animation
4. Loader shows for 2-3 seconds
5. Smooth fade-out
6. Back to normal

### Animations

All loaders animate smoothly at 60fps:
- **Spinner**: Smooth rotation
- **Dots**: Bouncing up and down in sequence
- **Pulse**: Pulsing with expanding ripples
- **Bar**: Sliding left to right with glow

### Responsive Behavior

On Mobile (< 768px):
- Single column layout
- Larger touch targets
- Adjusted spacing
- Smaller loader sizes
- Full-width buttons

### Visual Polish

- Smooth hover effects on all cards
- Subtle shadows that grow on hover
- Card "lift" animation on hover
- Smooth color transitions
- Gradient backgrounds throughout
- Professional typography
- Clean, modern aesthetic
